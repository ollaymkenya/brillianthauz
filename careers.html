Careers with Brilliant Hauz

Here at Brilliant Hauz, we are in the continual search for curious minds, creatives and passionate people who believe they have a dent to make and a mark to set on the earth.

Why work with us?

We consider Brilliant Hauz to be our home, and we recognize our coworkers as invaluable partners without whom we would achieve very little.

  • What if you could get a wonderful opportunity to join the brightest minds in the field of innovative breakthroughs and ideation?
  • Imagine if you found a team that truly cares for you?
  • A team that cares about your well-being and your growth and transformation more than anything else?
  • What if upon signing up you will get access to training material?
  • The key takeaway to all of that is that you can work from home!

That's exactly what we at Brilliant Hauz will offer you-- an exciting wonderful opportunity to be part of something great, with growth and improvement as part of your benefits and perks!

We are seeking highly motivated, PROACTIVE, self-driven, and professional individuals who are capable of efficiently completing their work.

What we value most is;

  • Reliability
  • Consistency
  • Teachability and humility.
  • Pro-activeness
  • A hunger for growth

Our five Purpose Principles are alive and well in our work culture and environment.

First, we believe in creating a family of people who are truly curious and happy to work and belong to a place- Brilliant Hauz in this case. You can only truly execute/implement when you truly belong.

Second, we believe in a high level of integrity. It's core to our company. We CANNOT allow anything that runs contrary to it. Including how we personally manage ourselves, our work ethic and personal performance standards, well as honesty with all our stakeholders and clients.

Third, we believe in being present, reliable, monstrous executors and implementors who always get it done. We don't give excuses, nor see how a task cannot be done. Instead we ask questions like, “How can this be done?”

Lastly, we believe in Lighting the Way, which means we lead by helping leaders choose what's best for their business and best for the world. We are trusted advisors.

We can't wait for you to join our team.

Here are the open positions available:

  • Market Researcher
  • Data Analyst
  • Resourceful Idea Person
  • Book Reader / Analyst

If you believe you can add value to our business and you would love to be part of our team, then please feel free to send an email to brillianthauz@gmail.com

Make sure you highlight what value you can add to us, however little or menial it may seem, we will listen!
